Hidden People Links - Converts explicit links to hidden people into [REDACTED] 1. Videos - Links video titles to their pages 2. Blog Posts - Links blog post titles to their pages (including embedded content) 3. People - Links person names to their pages, with conflict detection The people processing phase includes conflict detection to prevent double-linking. When a person's name appears within a blog post title that has already been linked, the person name linking is skipped. For example: - Blog post "Taylor Gerring Photos" gets linked first - When processing people, "Taylor Gerring" is detected as already being part of an existing blog post link, so it's skipped - This prevents creating nested tags like: Taylor Gerring Photos The system maintains proper priority: Hidden People > Videos > Blog Posts > People while ensuring clean, valid HTML output. {%- endcomment -%}
