People

STRATO Mercata is built by the team at BlockApps, pioneers in enterprise blockchain technology since 2014. Our team brings together decades of blockchain expertise, financial infrastructure knowledge, and a deep commitment to making digital assets more accessible and useful.

Many of our team members were involved in the early days of blockchain technology, contributing to foundational projects and bringing that experience to bear on creating a modern, secure, and user-friendly platform for earning on vaulted assets and accessing instant credit.

Below are profiles and biographies of the people building STRATO Mercata. We're continuously growing our team and expanding our capabilities to better serve our users and partners.
